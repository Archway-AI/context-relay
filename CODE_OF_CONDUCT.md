# Code Of Conduct

Context Relay contributors are expected to keep discussions technical,
respectful, and focused on building reliable agent infrastructure.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Assume good faith while still asking for evidence.
- Keep security-sensitive details private until maintainers can assess them.
- Respect different levels of experience with agent tooling.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory comments.
- Publishing private data, credentials, or exploit details without permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption of issues, reviews, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or
take other reasonable steps to protect the project and its contributors.

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers.
